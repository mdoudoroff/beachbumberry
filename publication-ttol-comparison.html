Total Tiki On-line vs. the Total Tiki app for iPhone and iPad

Here’s a summary of the differences between the Total Tiki On-line subscription service (introduced 2022) and the Total Tiki app for iPhone & iPad (introduced 2014):

  • web-based, works best on laptops or tablets, works adequately on phones (some features don’t translate to the small screen)
  • is independent to the Total Tiki app, and does NOT sync with it
  • contains all the recipes from the Total Tiki app (with more coming)
  • is far more data rich, including estimates of alcoholic strength, ABV, sweetness and acidity; new ways to sort and explore
  • has a vastly expanded keyword system for filtering the recipe database, including people (particular bartenders), places (particular bars), and flavors
  • rescale recipes for your glassware—you can even input your glassware collection for easy recall
  • automatic pour cost calculation for recipes, based on your own pricing data or aggregate (crowd-sourced) pricing data, across all the users
  • enter your own private recipes (requires higher subscription level) from scratch or customize recipes from the curated collection
    • data rich, just like the curated recipes
    • upload drink photos
  • recipe lists now include a quantity calculator for party planning: estimate what quantities you need of which ingredients based on how many servings of each drink you expect to deliver
  • same popular ingredient inventory system from Total Tiki—shows you which recipes you can make with what you have
  • expanded mixological ingredient database with full access to the ingredient hierarchy
  • favorite, flagged and (new!) shopping lists for ingredients, not just recipes
  • similar journaling feature from Total Tiki for annotating recipes and tracking which drinks you’ve liked or disliked
