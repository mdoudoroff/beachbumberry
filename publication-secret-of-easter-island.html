The Secret of Easter Island

Before the Bum discovered rum, he discovered the secret of Easter Island… and filmed it in an award-winning comedy short, shot on location in Rapa Nui with an old wind-up 16mm camera. Almost 25 years later, Sven Kirsten—author of The Book of Tiki, the tome that kick-started the retro Polynesian Pop culture revival—still calls it “the only true tiki movie ever made.” (And he should know.)
